## Overview

Dice Adventure is a puzzle game where players use dice mechanics to navigate through portals and solve challenges. Roll your way through increasingly complex levels!

## Gameplay

- 🎲 **Dice Mechanics** - Control your character by rolling dice
- 🌀 **Portal Puzzles** - Navigate through interconnected portals
- 🧩 **Progressive Difficulty** - Challenges that test your spatial reasoning
