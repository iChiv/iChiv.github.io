## Overview

**Cute Tower Defense** is a team project developed at MSG Studio, focused on building an approachable tower defense experience. For me, this project was primarily about **learning to design within a team structure** — aligning mechanic choices, sharing development responsibilities, and making sure gameplay decisions could be tested and iterated on collaboratively.

The project is currently in active development.

## My Role

Working within a team of eight, my contributions focused on gameplay system design and implementation, ensuring that the core tower defense mechanics had clear rules, readable feedback, and room for strategic depth.

## Current Status

This project is still in progress. As development continues, the focus is on refining the enemy progression curve, tower upgrade paths, and level pacing to create a satisfying strategic loop.
