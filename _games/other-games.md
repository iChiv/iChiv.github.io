## What I Gained from These Projects

GameJam and experimental prototypes are where I practice **rapid design iteration under constraints.** Each project forces a compressed design cycle: ideate, prototype, test, fail or adjust, and ship. The frequency and variety of these small projects have been essential in building my ability to evaluate an idea's viability quickly — a skill I carry into every larger project.
