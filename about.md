## Here is my story.

***Games are much more important than most people think.***

Twenty years ago, my dad brought home a computer and showed me a game about fighting aliens in a space base. All I felt at the time was excitement… so much amazing stuff!

Afterward, I learned that the game was called ***Half-Life*** and by this time I had accumulated thousands of hours of gaming time. Its rich and interesting worldview and interactive logic still amaze me today.Now I become a game designer and create wonderful journeys myself.
