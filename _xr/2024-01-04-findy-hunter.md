## Personal Contribution

I have a background in game design and production, so I took on the role of engineer on this project. Since most of my experience has been with Unity and Unreal Engine, my main job was to integrate art and sound resources, place them in the editor scene, and use Behavior scripts to solve logical problems such as dialog and option logic, animation, sound effect playback, and custom gesture recognition.
