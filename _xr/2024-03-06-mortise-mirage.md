## Overview

**Mortise Mirage** is a VR immersive 3D puzzle game with **ancient ink wash painting** and **traditional mortise and tenon woodwork** as its main cultural themes. Players experience the assembly process of traditional mortise and tenon wooden objects in an artistic landscape painting setting.

**Genre:** VR Puzzle  
**Platform:** Meta Quest  
**Tools:** Unity

## Highlights

- **Puzzle Assembly**: Unique immersive experience of being surrounded by puzzle pieces in 3D space
- **Educational Value**: Faithfully reproduces the appearance and structural features of woodworking heritage
- **Aesthetic Atmosphere**: Masterfully combines traditional art with immersive VR design
