## Overview

Ring Fit Adventure is Nintendo's innovative fitness RPG that successfully combines exercise with engaging gameplay mechanics.

## What Makes It Work

- 🏃 **Exercise as Gameplay** - Real workouts feel like game actions
- 📈 **Progression System** - RPG elements keep players motivated
- 🎯 **Accessibility** - Adjustable difficulty for all fitness levels
