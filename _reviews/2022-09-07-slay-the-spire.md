## Overview

Slay the Spire is a roguelike deck-building game that combines strategic card play with procedural generation. A masterclass in roguelike design.

## What Makes It Work

- 🃏 **Deck Building** - Build your deck as you progress through the spire
- ⚔️ **Strategic Combat** - Every card choice matters
- 🔄 **High Replayability** - Multiple characters and endless builds
